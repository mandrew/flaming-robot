Our Father

Our Father in heaven,
holy be your name.
Your Kingdom come,
Your will be done on earth as it is in heaven.
Give us today our daily bread
and forgive us our sins
as we forgive those who sin against us.
Save us from the time of trial and deliver us from evil.
Amen.

Tamara are i karawa

Tamara are i karawa
E na tabuaki Aram.
E na roko Ueam.
E na tauaki am Taeka i aon te aba
n ai aron tauana i karawa.
Anganira n te bong aei karara ae ti a tau iai.
Ao kab’arai are bure n ai arora ngkai
ti kab’arai aia bure akana a bure nakoira.
Ao tai kairira nakon te kaririaki,
ma kamaiuira man te buakaka.
Amen.

Ko te Pātere

E to mātou Matua i te rangi,
kia whakatapua tōu ingoa,
kia tae mai tōu rangatiratanga,
kia whakaritea tōu hiahia i te whenua
kia pērā anō i to te rangi.
Homai kia mātou äianei he taro mā mātou mō tēnei rā.
Whakakāhoretia o mātou hara, me mātou
e whakakore nei i ngā hara o te hunga e hara ana ki a mātou.
Kaua mātou e tukua kia whakawaia,
engari whakaorangia mātou i te kino.
Amene.
